415K plus chains on testnet. 254K users. $8.5M from Arrington, Fenbushi, Borderless, SNZ. The Tanssi acquisition quietly shipped the production plumbing. And the thesis from the June 17 tweet is correct: the first truly autonomous agent will want a chain of its own. Canopy is building for that day.

These four partnerships get the architecture in front of the people who need it. No ads, no content campaigns. Just the right conversations with the right teams.

What you get
75+ existing chains and every new one they launch. Zero marginal cost. Every chain Caldera ships becomes a Canopy chain by default, inheriting your validator network instead of bootstrapping from zero.
